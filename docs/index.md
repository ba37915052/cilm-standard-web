# Component Integrity & Lifecycle Management (CILM)

The Component Integrity & Lifecycle Management (CILM) framework is an analytical and operational methodology designed for electronic component supply chain risk management, documentation integrity verification, counterfeit-risk mitigation, and lifecycle-level component verification.

CILM is structured to provide an evidence-based methodological infrastructure for supplier intelligence and component lifecycle assessment. It operationalizes principles of component verification within the supply chain, facilitating structured procurement documentation review and trusted sourcing workflows.

This platform serves as a technical repository documenting the CILM architecture, empirical foundations, and practical applications within the context of the U.S. electronic component market.

## Scope and Application

The framework provides structured analytical models and operational workflows for:

- **Supply Chain Risk Intelligence:** Systematic assessment of supplier risk profiles.
- **Documentation Integrity:** Verification protocols for procurement and lifecycle documentation.
- **Counterfeit-Risk Mitigation:** Methodological guidelines for identifying and mitigating risks associated with anomalous electronic components.
- **Verification Operations:** Structured approaches to lifecycle-level component verification.
