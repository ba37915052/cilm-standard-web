# About the Originator

The CILM framework was developed by an electronic component supply chain risk intelligence professional with 27 years of experience in the field. The originator is a Senior Member of the IEEE and a member of the Association for Supply Chain Management (ASCM).

The framework represents a synthesis of extensive operational experience and analytical research in supply chain risk management, component verification, and documentation integrity within the electronic components sector.
