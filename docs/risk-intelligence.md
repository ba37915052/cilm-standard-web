# Risk Intelligence Models

The supply chain risk intelligence component of the CILM framework utilizes analytical models to assess and quantify risks associated with electronic component suppliers and procurement channels.

## Empirical Foundation: CILM-IRI Dataset

The risk modeling framework is empirically supported by the **CILM-IRI dataset (N=67)**. This dataset provides the quantitative foundation for the risk intelligence models and is archived under **IEEE DataPort DOI: 10.21227/34y3-zj88**.

## CIRS Scoring Framework

The CILM framework utilizes the Component Intelligence Risk Score (CIRS) to quantify supplier and supply chain risk. CIRS is a 5-indicator weighted composite scoring framework, resulting in a risk index ranging from **0.00 to 1.00**.

The analytical model integrates multiple dimensions of supplier performance and documentation integrity to generate a structured risk assessment.
